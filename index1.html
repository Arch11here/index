How to get started in calisthenics?

The following is a simple way to get started in calisthenics.

The basic exercises are the most common exercise that everyone knows, like Push ups, Pull ups, squats.These are the foundational exercises in calisthenics.

Equipments needed for beginners: You will need a floor and something that you can use to do pull ups, that's it.

The following table will help you with determining how to form a plan for yourself in accordance with your lifestyle.P.S.=Only for beginners:

Exercises Sets & Reps Remarks
Push-ups 1 set;Until failure Do one set of as many reps as you can.
Pull-ups 1 set;Until failure Do one set of as many reps as you can.
Squats 1 set;Until failure Do one set of as many reps as you can.

Do all the three described exercises for 1 month as a beginner.

The following warm up session should be done before the workout:

  1. Tap yourself all over the body, like literally everywhere and dont be scared its hard to hurt yourself.
  2. Do 10-20 jumping jacks.

The following should be done after the workout:

  1. Stretching (How to stretch is in later pages).
  2. Reverse Breathing (How to do reverse breathing is in later pages).

The following should be keep in mind:

  1. last food that you eat should be 2-3 hours before the workout.
  2. Things like energy drinks are not considered in above mentioned point.
  3. Drink water before workout but not too much, 250ml should be more than enough.
