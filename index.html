CALISTHENICS: Foundation

INTRODUCTION

A brief introduction about calisthenics and why it should be the mainstream form of exercise in future.

Calisthenics means using ur own body to create resistance with help of no to minimal outside objects, to perform an exercise.Intentions behind doing these types of exercise can be strength gain, fat loss, flexbility improvement, mobility improvement.

Key Features of calisthenics:

  • No need of weights & equipments.
  • No need of too much space.
  • Modification in exercises are available for more personalisation.
  • Faster activation of muscle groups.
  • Accessible for every age group.
  • Reduced risk of accidents and mishappenings.
  • Easy for beginners.
